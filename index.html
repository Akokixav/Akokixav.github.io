Learn More

Most organizations, including consumer package goods companies, retailers, tech companies, and B2B companies, often want or need third-party assistance to get the best possible business results. Conducting market research and business analysis, developing relevant and powerful insights, analyzing and optimizing how to go to market, and creating and executing winning strategies and plans can be incredibly challenging, while also running the business. And, budgets are always tight. The Marriott School Marketing Lab offers a solution. They provide professional caliber consulting at 1/3 to 1/2 the cost of a typical consulting firm. The Marketing Lab hand picks the best and brightest under-graduate and MBA marketing students. They then work in teams under the hands-on direction of world class faculty and C-level business executives from blue-chip, multi-national companies to deliver for clients. The net result is professional caliber work at a fraction of the cost.
